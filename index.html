About Us

Ramchandra Kolhapuri Chappal has been serving customers for over 25 years, bringing authentic handmade chappals straight from the artisans of Kolhapur. Our mission is to preserve this timeless art while providing unmatched comfort and quality.

Discover traditional Kolhapuri chappals handcrafted in the heart of Kolhapur, Maharashtra. Made from genuine leather and crafted by skilled artisans, our footwear reflects the rich heritage of local craftsmanship.

As a proud Marathi entrepreneur, our mission is to preserve our cultural legacy and support local business through sustainable handcraft practices.

Every pair of Kolhapuri chappals is a symbol of tradition, quality, and comfort. Explore our unique collection today!
